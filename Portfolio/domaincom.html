It All Starts With a Great Domain!

Background

As a former marketing coordinator at Domain.com, I had to stay ahead of our competition by brainstorming creative content for our social media accounts, branding campaigns, and conference booth activities. I also worked closely with our web designer to create graphics and started noticing that companies like Name.com, GoDaddy, NameCheap were redesigning their websites to stay up to date with current web design trends. This was always in the back of my mind and when I started learning how front end web development in my free time, I decided to redesign the website as a fun web design project.

My role

I prototyped an improved version of the front end of Domain.com's website.

Process

I had to work through the current flow of the website and track all the details of partnership branding on the homepage. These were crucial and had to stay on the page in the way they were placed.

Once I had my vision, I created a prototype of the app using Photoshop.

I will be meeting with the Marketing Director and Senior Marketing Manager of Domain.com to show them the live, working version of the site to get feedback and work on further improvements.

Outcome

The project is a work in progress and will eventually be live so stay tuned!

Methods and Tools

Adobe Photoshop, Paper prototypes, Dropbox
